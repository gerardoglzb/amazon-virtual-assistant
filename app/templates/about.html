- HOW TO USE -


The Amazon Virtual Assistant helps you keep track of whatever products you wish to buy next on Amazon with just a few clicks, in order to know when their prices drop below a certain point.


Just add the product's url into our system (as well as your preferred price for said product) and we'll do the rest for you, by notifying you as soon as we see the price is right!


We're currently only working with the Amazon websites for the following locations: USA, Canada, Australia, UK.
